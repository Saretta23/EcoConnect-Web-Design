Our Journey

Since its inception, EcoConnect has been on a mission to foster a greener and more sustainable world. Our journey began in 2010 when a group of passionate environmental advocates came together with a shared vision of creating positive change.

2010

EcoConnect is founded by a group of environmental enthusiasts who believe in the power of collective action.

2012

Our first community cleanup event gathers volunteers from across the city, kickstarting our efforts to create cleaner public spaces.

2014

We launch our "Plastic-Free City" campaign, partnering with local businesses to promote the use of sustainable alternatives to plastic.

2016

EcoConnect's innovative "Green Energy for All" initiative brings solar panels to low-income neighborhoods, providing clean energy solutions to underserved communities.

2018

Our partnerships with schools lead to the integration of environmental education into curricula, inspiring the next generation of eco-conscious leaders.

2020

We celebrate a major achievement by planting our one-millionth tree, contributing to reforestation efforts and combatting climate change.
